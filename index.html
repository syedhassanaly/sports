Welcome to Hassan's Cricket Hockey and our huge range of cricket equipment in our cricket shop! We offer the very best range of cricket equipment from all the world’s leading brands including including Gray Nicolls, Gunn & Moore, Adidas, Kookaburra and some of the very best UK hand made bat brands in Chase, Hell 4 Leather, Salix and Newbery. Other brands you can find in store are the two leafing helmet cricket helmet manufacturers Masuri and Shrey and others brands including New Balance, Aero, Asics, , and many others plus the best cricket socks in the world from 1000 Mile.

You can search our huge range of cricket bats with sizes from 0 up to full size short handles to suit all budgets and abilities. As well as bats we have a huge range of cricket helmets, cricket bags, Cricket Footwear, Cricket Protection Cricket Clothing and Wicket Keeping. Quite simply if you need it we have it!

In addition to the basic equipment we also stock a huge range of Cricket Accessories which include Umpire Equipment for everything an umpire would need. We also have a hand Cricket at Home section which is packed full of ideas for playing at home. Gift Ideas, Cricket Sets, Balls and a page dedicated to Cricket Coaching
